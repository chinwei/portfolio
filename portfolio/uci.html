UC Irvine UX design competition entry

Traffic Simulation Tool

I was shortlisted in a UX design competition organized by University of California Irvine, as part of a crowd sourcing study.

Overview

The task of the competition was to design a traffic simulation tool for students of Civil Engineering students, to allow them to experiment and understand the impact of traffic light timings on traffic conditions.
