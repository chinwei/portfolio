Design Rationale

A poster illustrating one of the possible ways to make the microwave controls more usable. Considering they are one of the most ubiquitous kitchen appliances around, people still have to wrestle with various buttons just to get it to heat up their food. I came up with a simpler alternative for those who doesn't want to go through the user manual in order to get the microwave to reheat their food.
