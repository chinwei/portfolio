Mallia Express yourself like never before

Bringing print to life

As part of the campaign to market Legrand's launch of their Mallia line up of switches, we built this interactive website to amplify the campaign message through digital channels. I designed and built all interactivity for the website.

Lively Switches

Brisk staggered transitions showcase the selection of colored baseplates available while imparting a sense of light-hearted elegance.

Switch Around

Mallia switches feature baseplates and rockers with a large variety of colors and styles. Here, users are able to experiment with different combinations of switches and backdrops.

Sultry Curves

The interactive video player allow users to explore the unique curved profile of the switch at any angle, for both glossy and matte finishes.

Boundless Combinations

This configurator allow users to explore the available combinations in the series, and to save the favorites for later use.
